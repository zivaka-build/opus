We are trying. A drop in the desert but we will overcome
AMPHAN brought with it the second-highest wind speeds to hit the Indian sub-continent since Independence. The South of Bengal bore the brunt. Lives have been severely disrupted, Homes lost, Villages decimated, possessions washed away, livelihoods destroyed. Agricultural land with saltwater contamination remains unproductive for a few years. Sources of future income are lost as well. These are COVID times and it seems that all the forces are conspiring against the villagers.
We Care Initiative, an NGO based out of Kolkata has been carrying out relief work in the Sundarbans, so that more villagers may be benefited.
